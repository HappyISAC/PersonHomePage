Biography


I am a Lecturer at the School of Computer Science and Information Engineering, Hefei University of Technology (HFUT). I received my Ph.D. in the School of Cyber Science and Technology, University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), supervised by Prof. Nenghai Yu, Prof. Honggang Hu, and Prof. Bin Liu. During my doctoral period, I receive a CSC scholarship, and I am guided by Prof. Lu Su from Purdue University and Chang Wen Chen from University at Buffalo to conduct scientific research. Before starting my Ph.D. study, I received my Bachelor's degree from Anhui University (AHU) in 2017, supervised by Prof. Linsheng Huang. Since 2022, I also conduct research as an exchange scholar under the guidance of Prof. Zhi Liu in The University of Electro-Communications and Prof. Yusheng Ji in National Institute of Informatics. I am broadly interested in Human-computer interaction, Wireless sensing, Wireless communication, Computer Vision, Cyber security, and Internet of Things.
